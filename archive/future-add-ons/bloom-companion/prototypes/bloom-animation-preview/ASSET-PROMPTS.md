# Bloom animation asset prompts

These assets were generated with OpenAI's built-in image generation, then chroma-keyed and normalized into fixed 4-by-2 sprite sheets. Every final frame uses a 512-by-512 transparent cell, one centerline, one ground baseline, and a consistent face scale.

Shared character prompt: Bloom is the same cute young golden-tan sloth from SlothBloom Sprouts, with a cream heart-shaped face mask and belly, large dark-brown eyes, pink cheeks, three hair tufts, rounded paws, soft storybook watercolor texture, clean outline, front-facing proportions, and no text. Render exactly eight complete poses in a 4-by-2 grid on solid magenta, with generous separation and no cropped body parts.

- Idle/blink: arms resting naturally at the sides; open eyes, gradual integrated eyelid close, fully closed eyes, gradual reopen, ending on the identical neutral pose. No pasted eyelid shapes.
- Wave: neutral pose, one arm extends outward, rises, makes a small natural side-to-side wrist/forearm wave, then returns to the identical neutral pose. The body and feet stay planted.
- Eat: Bloom holds one clearly visible palm-sized strawberry, raises it naturally to the mouth, takes one bite, chews, swallows, lowers the paw, and returns to neutral. No floating or tiny berry.
- Sleep: Bloom grows drowsy, slowly closes both eyes, sits, lies on a gray pillow, and is covered by a soft gray blanket, ending in a comfortable fully asleep pose.
- Celebrate: Bloom crouches, raises both arms overhead, jumps vertically with both hands up, lands, settles, and returns to the identical neutral pose. No horizontal travel or camera zoom.

The Wake animation deliberately reuses the Sleep sheet in reverse so the body, pillow, blanket, scale, and anchor match exactly.
